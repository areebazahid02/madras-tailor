About Madras Tailor's

Welcome to Madras Tailor, where quality, precision, and style come together.We specialize in premium gents' tailoring, offering custom stitching, alterations, and perfectly fitted garments for every occasion.

Founder (1967)

Master Nanne Kha

Madras Tailor’s was founded in 1967 by Master Nanne Kha, A Master craftsman known for his dedication, skill, and passion for perfect tailoring.

From traditional wear to modern outfits, Madras Tailor’s has always focused on precision, comfort, and timeless style.

Present CEO

Master Zahid Khan

Today, Madras Tailor’s is proudly led by Zahid Khan, who carries forward the legacy with a modern vision.

With innovation, quality, and customer trust at the core, Zahid Khan is guiding Madras Tailor’s into a new era of style.
